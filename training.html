Specialized Rescue Training

  • February 8, 2018

Sponsorship Form

SAMSAR is seeking community leaders to sponsor area first responders to attend a specialized technical rescue class coming to Tucson this spring, March 24, 25, & 26.

There will be a 1 day classroom instruction and a 2 day hands on that teaches the students technical rescue concepts unique to large animal incidents including scene management, rescue, capture, recover, and specialized equipment techniques. It covers trailer vehicle incidents, trail riding incidents, and stable incidents. The class is designed for first responding agency personnel keeping themselves and the animals involved safe during an incident extraction. The course curriculum is well established and approved as a California State Marshall Technical Rescue Fire Course. It has been offered to Arizona first responders since 2008. The class will be held at the S.A.R.A. building : 5900 N. Sabino Canyon Rd.

The course curriculum is well established and approved as a California State Marshall Technical Rescue Fire Course.

Sponsors can make a TAX DEDUCTIBLE DONATION to Southern Arizona Mounted Search and Rescue via check sent to the address above, or contact Colleen Leon 520-990-7499 for any Visa Credit Card donation.

The 3 day class cost is $375.00 per person. Sponsors can either delegate an agency or person they would like to sponsor or leave it up to the Host Party, SAMSAR, to determine the highest priority responding agency to get the most coverage of trained individuals in the class.

Any and all donations accepted. SAMSAR is a recognized 501C3 charitable organization. Our number is 27-4296883.

To thank sponsors, we will include promotional material in the instruction booklet, highlight your sponsorship on our web page and Facebook fan page, and will post any promotional material you might supply in the classroom.

Please help us ensure the highest quality of large animal rescue here in the Tucson community
